# Carma Cannabis Insurance Overview

## About Carma Cannabis Insurance

A managing general agency (MGA) designed specifically for the cannabis industry. We cut through the red tape and help brokers place coverage where others won’t. From dispensaries to cultivators, testing labs to delivery services, we offer real solutions tailored to the risks cannabis operators actually face.

With operations in every cannabis-legal state, Carma gives brokers the tools to serve their clients with confidence. Because in an industry this complex, expertise matters—and no one understands cannabis risk better than those who’ve lived it.

We make cannabis insurance simple, with tailored coverage for operators in every legal market, in all 50 states and Canada. No red tape, just real protection.
